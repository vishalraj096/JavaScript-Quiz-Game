Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) :-

1) How do I start playing the quiz game?

Ans: To start playing the quiz game, simply click on the "Start" button on the homepage.


2) How much time do I have to answer each question?

Ans: There is no time limit in this game. You can take as much time as you want.


3) Can I skip a question if I don't know the answer?

Ans: Yes, skipping questions is allowed. But you should answer each question before moving on to the next one. However, if you're unsure about an answer, you can make an educated guess.


4) How are scores calculated in the quiz game?

Ans: Scores are calculated based on the number of correct answers in the quiz game.


5) Can I compete with other players in the quiz game?

Ans: No, you can't! We don't have a leaderboard yet where you can view your ranking among other players.


6) How can I contact customer support for any issues or inquiries?

Ans: If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please visit the "Contact Us" page and fill out the form provided. Our customer support team will respond to you as soon as possible.
